**LuckyDay** is a fair, transparent, and automated lottery dApp running on the **Arc Testnet**.

Participate in daily draws using **USDC** or **EURC** stablecoins. The prize pools are separate, meaning you win the currency you play with!

## üìñ How to Play

Follow this step-by-step guide to participate in the LuckyDay lottery:

### 1. Connect your Wallet

* Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right corner.

* **Metamask** will verify if you are on the correct network (**Arc Testnet**). If not, the dApp will ask you to switch networks automatically.

* **Sign In:** For security reasons, you will be asked to sign a message to confirm you are the owner of the wallet.

### 2. Get Testnet Tokens

To play, you need **USDC** or **EURC** on the Arc Testnet.

* You can request free test tokens from the official [Circle Faucet](https://faucet.circle.com/).

* Make sure to select **Arc Testnet** as the network.

### 3. Choose Your Pool

LuckyDay has two separate prize pools:

* üîµ **USDC Pool:** Ticket costs **1.00 USDC**.

* üü¢ **EURC Pool:** Ticket costs **1.00 EURC**.

Select the currency you want to use in the **"Get Ticket"** card.

### 4. Buy a Ticket

* Click the **"Enter Draw"** button.

* **First time?** You will need to approve the contract to spend your 1.00 token (Approve transaction).

* **Confirm:** Confirm the purchase transaction in your wallet.

* Once confirmed, your ticket count will update (e.g., `1 / 10 max`).

### 5. Wait for the Draw

* The countdown timer shows when the next automated draw will happen.

* The draw is executed automatically by the system.

* **Fairness:** The winner is selected pseudo-randomly based on blockchain data.

### 6. Check Winners

* Scroll down to the **"Winners History"** section.

* You can see the latest winners, the prize amount, and the currency won.

* Click on any winner card to view the transaction details on **Arcscan** (Block Explorer) for full transparency.
